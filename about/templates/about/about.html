About Woodford 3D

Woodford 3D printing is a small Irish business focused on providing affordable, reliable and personal 3D printing services. Founded in Cavan, in late 2020, we have been providing a wide range of customers both locally and abroad with our own products and our custom printing service.

These days, Woodford 3D is proud to exclusively use Prusa Research's i3 MK3S+ printers. These printers are widely regarded as one of the best printers on the market and have consistently won almost every award across the industry. The realiability and high performance of these printers helps us ensure that customers are able to get the highest quality results.
